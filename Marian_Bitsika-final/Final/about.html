About:BIO

Hi! I am Marian Bitsika. I am a Greek-American, thirteen year old girl that lives in Athens, Greece. I attend ACS Athens and am a John Hopkins scholar. As I am an eighth grade student and graduated Middle School with an outstanding academic performance award in 7 out of 8 of my classes. At school I am part of three teams, two sports teams and debate. I have been a member of my school's volleyball team since 6th grade and this year I was team captain on the swimming team. I am fluent in two languages English and Greek and am currently learning Spanish.

In my free time I enjoy reading, playing sports and going to the movies. My favorite books are The Hunger Games, my favorite TV comedy series is The Big Bang Theory and my favorite movie is The Life of Pi. Since I am next to the sea I practice and compete in multiple water-sports such as kayaking, sailing, wind-surfing and swimming. An activity I like, but don't do very often, is to do escape rooms. I love to travel and hope someday to see all of the world's wonders.

P.S. Something a lot of people don't know about me is that I am afraid of pigeons. (Pigeonphobia as my friends call it.)
