HTML and CSS Honors

First there was looking at webpages. Then there was understanding. And now I am excelling at making them.

What I made

In this class I learned how to make basic to intermediate websites using HTML and CSS. I made various websites demonstrating my daily progress. In a few cases I created images with shapes I had managed to create through these two programming languages.

What I learned

In this class I learned how to hyperlink text and redirect the page to other external and internal webpages and or websites. I learned how to appropriately style my webpages and add effects to them aswell. Positioning content and making shapes did trouble me at first, but after a few trials I managed to achieve my desired look. I also learned how to make online forms, use the transform property to alter the appearance of shapes and create shapes in 2D and 3D. I also learned the bootstrap property, how to add media and a tiny bit of JavaScript.

What I enjoyed

Out of everything I loved styling my webpages by adding effects. Making forms was an assignment I enjoyed.

How I might use these skills in the future

I will definitely continue making webpages and websites in the future. In the future to come I am not sure how I will place my webpage making skills to practice, but I am positive that I will make websites for school. Even before school starts, during this summer, I will be making webpages and websites over the summer for my summer homework assignments. During school, aside computer class, I will be making webpages for various school projects such as the science fair and final projects.
